## ğŸ¯ Pattern Combination Guide

### Trap Combinations

**Classic Trap:**
- Kick: Classic
- Hi-Hat: Closed + Trap Roll (last 2 bars)

**UK Drill:**
- Kick: Drill or Rolling
- Hi-Hat: Drill Roll

**Boom-Bap:**
- Kick: Breakbeat
- Hi-Hat: Half Time

**Modern Trap:**
- Kick: Double or Syncopated
- Hi-Hat: Closed + Open accents

### Techno Combinations

**Peak-Time Techno:**
- Kick: Four Floor or Hard
- Hi-Hat: Closed 8ths + Open accents

**Minimal Techno:**
- Kick: Minimal
- Hi-Hat: Minimal or Offbeat

**Industrial Techno:**
- Kick: Industrial
- Hi-Hat: Industrial + 16ths

**Acid Techno:**
- Kick: Acid
- Hi-Hat: 16ths + Rolling
