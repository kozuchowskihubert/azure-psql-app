Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.
What happens to my tracks if I downgrade?
Your tracks will remain in your account, but you won't be able to create new ones beyond your plan's limit. You'll still have access to download all your existing tracks.

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, contact us and we'll process your refund.
